MRA 2025 TAX RATES (Quick Reference)
================================================================================

PAYE (Annual Income Barème):
  0 to 390,000 MUR:       0% tax-free
  390,001 to 700,000:     10% (on amount above 390k)
  700,001+:               15% (on amount above 700k)
  
  HOW TO APPLY:
  1. Multiply monthly gross × 12 (annualize)
  2. Apply barème based on annual total
  3. Divide annual tax by 12 (monthly PAYE)

CSG (Contribution Sociale Généralisée):
  If monthly gross ≤ 50,000 MUR:    1.5% deduction
  If monthly gross > 50,000 MUR:    3.0% deduction
  
  KEY: Threshold is at 50,000 MUR exactly

NSF (National Savings Fund):
  Employee deduction:     1.0% of gross
  Monthly insurable cap:  228,000 MUR
  (If gross > cap, apply 1% to cap only)

SAMPLE CALCULATION (50k/month employee):
  Gross:              50,000
  Annual:             600,000 (50k × 12)
  PAYE bracket:       390k-700k (10%)
  Taxable:            600k - 390k = 210k
  Annual tax:         210k × 10% = 21,000
  Monthly PAYE:       21,000 ÷ 12 = 1,750
  
  CSG (at threshold): 50,000 × 1.5% = 750
  NSF (under cap):    50,000 × 1.0% = 500
  
  Total deductions:   1,750 + 750 + 500 = 3,000
  Net salary:         50,000 - 3,000 = 47,000
