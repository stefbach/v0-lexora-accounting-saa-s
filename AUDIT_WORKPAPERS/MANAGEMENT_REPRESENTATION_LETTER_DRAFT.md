## INTRODUCTION

This letter is provided in connection with your audit of the financial statements of [Client Name] for the year ended 31 December 2025, and the related notes to the financial statements, for the purpose of expressing an opinion on whether the financial statements are presented fairly, in all material respects, in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) and Mauritian accounting standards.

## MANAGEMENT CERTIFICATIONS & REPRESENTATIONS

### 1. COMPLETENESS OF TRANSACTIONS & EVENTS

We represent that:

- [ ] **All financial transactions and events** that occurred during the year ended 31 December 2025 have been **recorded in the accounting records** maintained by the Company.

- [ ] We are not aware of any transactions or events that were intentionally omitted from the accounting records or financial statements.

- [ ] All accruals, provisions, and liabilities (known, estimated, or contingent) have been **appropriately recognized or disclosed** in the financial statements.

- [ ] We confirm the **completeness of disclosure** regarding related party transactions, both in terms of identification and quantification.

**Supporting Documentation Reviewed:**
- [ ] General ledger detail
- [ ] Journal entries (monthly review)
- [ ] Invoices and supporting documents
- [ ] Payroll records and calculations
- [ ] Bank statements and reconciliations

### 2. ACCURACY & VALUATION

We represent that:

- [ ] All amounts recorded in the financial statements are **accurate and properly valued** in accordance with the applicable accounting framework (IFRS/Mauritian standards).

- [ ] The measurement bases for assets, liabilities, revenue, and expenses are appropriate and consistently applied.

- [ ] All account balances have been **reconciled to supporting documentation**, and any exceptions have been resolved.

- [ ] Accruals and provisions are **based on current best estimates** of future cash flows or obligations.

- [ ] **Depreciation and amortization** have been calculated using appropriate methods and useful life assumptions.

- [ ] **Inventory** (if applicable) has been **valued using appropriate methods** (FIFO, weighted average, or other documented method) and includes a provision for obsolescence if necessary.

- [ ] **Accounts receivable** have been reviewed for **collectibility**, and an appropriate allowance for doubtful debts has been recorded.

---

### 3. AUTHORIZATION & APPROVAL

We represent that:

- [ ] All transactions and account entries have been **authorized and approved** according to established authorization matrices and approval thresholds.

- [ ] The **segregation of duties** (authorization, recording, custody, reconciliation) has been maintained and is evidenced by system controls.

- [ ] **Management override of controls** has not occurred, and no instances of circumventing established control procedures have come to our attention.

- [ ] No unauthorized transactions or manual journal entries have been recorded that circumvent normal approval procedures.

---

### 4. FRAUD & IRREGULARITIES

We represent that:

- [ ] We have disclosed to you **all known or suspected frauds, embezzlements, or irregularities** involving management, employees, or third parties that could materially affect the financial statements.

- [ ] We have disclosed all instances where **controls have been bypassed or overridden** by management or employees.

- [ ] **No fraudulent financial reporting** has been attempted or completed during the year.

- [ ] No **misappropriation of assets** has occurred that has not been recorded in the financial statements or disclosed to you.

- [ ] We are not aware of any **whistleblower complaints** or regulatory investigations that could affect the financial statements or audit opinion.

---

- [ ] All **MRA filings** (PAYE, NSF, CSG, corporate income tax) have been completed accurately and on time.
