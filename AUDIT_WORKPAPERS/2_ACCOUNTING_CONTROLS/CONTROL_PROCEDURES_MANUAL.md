# WP 2.2 - CONTROL PROCEDURES MANUAL
**Workpaper Reference:** WP 2.2.1  
**Period Ended:** 31 December 2025  
**Prepared By:** Finance & Compliance | **Date:** 22 May 2026

## OBJECTIVE
Document all key accounting control procedures in place during FY2025.

## CONTROL PROCEDURES BY MODULE

### GL Entry Controls
- All GL entries require approval per authorization matrix
- Manual entries: Reviewed by Finance Manager within 5 business days
- System-generated entries: Reviewed and verified monthly at period close
- All entries supported by source documentation (invoice, payroll, bank statement)

### Invoice Processing Controls
- Invoices created by Accounting Clerk in system
- Approval routing:
  - < MUR 10,000: Finance Manager/Finance Administrator approval
  - MUR 10,000-50,000: Finance Manager approval
  - > MUR 50,000: CEO/Managing Director approval
- Posted to GL upon approval (GL post date within 3 business days)

### Payroll Controls
- Payroll prepared by Finance Manager
- Reviewed and approved monthly by Finance Manager
- Verified against HR records (headcount, salary changes)
- PAYE withholding calculated per MRA tax tables
- NSF contributions calculated per schedule
- GL posting reviewed monthly for accuracy

### Bank Reconciliation Controls
- Monthly reconciliations prepared within 10 days of month-end
- Reviewed and approved by Finance Manager
- Outstanding items investigated and aged
- Reconciling items followed up within 30 days
