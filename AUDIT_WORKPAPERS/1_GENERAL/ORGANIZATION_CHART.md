## CHART OF AUTHORITIES & APPROVALS

### For Financial Transactions (DDS)
- **Invoices < MUR 10,000:** Head Accountant approval
- **Invoices MUR 10,000-50,000:** Finance Manager approval
- **Invoices > MUR 50,000:** CEO approval
- **Bank Transfers:** CFO + Finance Manager (dual approval)

### For Financial Transactions (OCC)
- **Invoices < MUR 5,000:** Finance Administrator approval
- **Invoices MUR 5,000-25,000:** Practice Manager approval
- **Invoices > MUR 25,000:** Medical Director approval
- **Bank Transfers:** Practice Manager + Finance Admin
